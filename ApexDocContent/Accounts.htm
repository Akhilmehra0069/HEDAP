Account Related Classes

NPSP supports 4 distinct types of Accounts.

  • Organizational Accounts - these are normal Salesforce Accounts, which typically represent a business, organization, or government agency.
  • Administrative Accounts - these are university administrative Accounts, where each corresponds one-to-one with a Contact record.
  • Household Accounts - these are Accounts whose Contacts are all members of the same Household

The Account Model that is specified in the Settings dictates which of the non-Organizational Accounts are automatically created and used&npsp; when a Contact is created. Administrative and Household Accounts are recommended as the preferred Account Model.

  • ACCT_IndividualAccounts_TDTM - Trigger Handler on Contacts, that manages creating the appropriate Account for Contacts, when they are created or updated.
  • CAO_Constants - Constants and helper methods used with the Account Model.
