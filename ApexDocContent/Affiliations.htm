Affiliations Related Classes

Affiliations management is made up of two trigger handler classes and a helper class.

  • AFFL_Affiliations_TDTM - Handles affiliation management for contact, account, and affiliation triggers.
  • AFFL_AccRecordType_TDTM - Supports changing the record type of an account.
  • AFFL_MultiRecordType_TDTM - Handles affiliations of different record types, with one primary per type.
